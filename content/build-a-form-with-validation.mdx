## Requirements

Build a registration form with the following fields and rules:
- **Name** — required, minimum 3 characters
- **Email** — required, must match a valid email pattern
- **Password** — required, minimum 8 characters with at least one number
- **Confirm Password** — must match the Password field
- Show inline error messages below each field after the field is blurred
- Disable the submit button until all fields are valid
- Reset the form after successful submission
